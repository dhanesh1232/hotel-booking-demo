About Us

A simple, honest stay in Kokar.

Hotel Sai Leela is a small, family-friendly hotel built around three values: cleanliness, calm stay, and straightforward service—so you can focus on your visit, not your room.

Sai Leela was started with a simple thought: most guests coming to Kokar Ranchi don’t need luxury—they just need a clean, safe, and peaceful place to rest between travel and darshan.

Instead of adding heavy decor and hidden charges, we focus on basics done right: fresh beds, working AC, hot water, Wi‑Fi, and a team that genuinely tries to help with timings, local guidance and small requests.

Over the years, we’ve hosted families, working professionals, solo travellers and small groups who prefer a practical stay near the station and temple, without confusion on rates or facilities.

This website is a simple extension of that same approach—clear information, no over-promising, and an easy way to connect with our team before you book.

What you can expect here

  • • Walkable / short-ride distance from main transport points
  • • AC / Non‑AC rooms with daily housekeeping
  • • 24×7 front desk support for check‑ins and basic queries
  • • Nearby food, tea/coffee and cab/auto options
